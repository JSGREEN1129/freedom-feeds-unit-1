Our Mission

Freedom Feeds is committed to supporting veterans and their families who are struggling with the challenges of everyday life due to the lasting effects of war. Whether facing the loss of a loved one, coping with severe mental health issues, or adjusting to life with a physical injury, many veterans and their families experience immense hardship.

We provide essential food donations, ensuring that no veteran or their family goes hungry. Beyond meals, we offer practical assistance, helping to ease financial strain and improve overall well-being. Our mission is to stand by those who have served, offering the respect, care, and support they deserve in their time of need.

Sophie's Story

Sophie’s life changed forever when her husband, a dedicated serviceman, returned home from deployment. The man she loved had endured the unimaginable, and the weight of war left deep scars—ones that couldn’t be seen but were impossible to ignore. His struggle with severe mental health challenges meant that Sophie not only became his carer but also the sole provider for their two young children.

Some days were harder than others. Between hospital visits, therapy sessions, and trying to maintain a sense of normality for her children, Sophie often found herself overwhelmed. Financial struggles added another layer of stress—balancing rent, energy bills, school supplies, and basic necessities felt like an uphill battle.

That’s when Freedom Feeds stepped in. Through food packages and essential grants, the organisation ensured that Sophie’s family never had to worry about their next meal or keeping their home warm during winter. It wasn’t just about financial support—it was about dignity, relief, and knowing that someone cared.

Thanks to Freedom Feeds, Sophie’s family has regained a sense of stability. While the challenges remain, they no longer have to face them alone.

Impact So Far

At Freedom Feeds, we are dedicated to supporting war veterans and their families by providing essential resources. So far, we have helped over 1,500 veterans, delivering more than 150,000 nutritious meals to those in need.

We have also distributed over £70,000 in grants to help families cover energy bills, purchase clothing, purchase household appliances, and other essentials. Every donation enables us to extend our support, ensuring veterans and their families receive the care and assistance they deserve. Together, we are making a real difference in the lives of those who have served.
